First Aid Tips

Essential guidelines to help you manage emergencies and injuries effectively.

Why First Aid is Important

Knowing first aid can save lives. Quick and proper action can prevent the situation from worsening before professional help arrives.

First Aid Tips

1. Cardiac Arrest

If someone has a cardiac arrest, perform CPR immediately. Call for help and use an AED if available.

2. Bleeding

If someone is bleeding heavily, apply pressure to the wound to stop the flow of blood. Elevate the legs if the person is in shock.

3. Burns

For minor burns, run cool water over the affected area for at least 10 minutes. For severe burns, cover with a clean, non-stick cloth and seek medical attention immediately.

4. Choking

If someone is choking, perform the Heimlich maneuver to expel the object blocking the airway. Call for help if needed.
